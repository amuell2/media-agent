## Creator Verification Requirements

To become a verified creator on StreamVerse:

1. **Identity Verification**
   - Government-issued ID
   - Business registration (for companies)
   - Tax documentation

2. **Content Requirements**
   - Minimum 30 days of streaming history
   - No community guideline violations
   - Original content only

3. **Engagement Thresholds**
   - Minimum 1,000 subscribers
   - Minimum 10,000 total views
   - At least 4 streams per month

4. **Technical Requirements**
   - Minimum 720p streaming capability
   - Stable internet connection (10+ Mbps upload)
   - Professional audio quality
