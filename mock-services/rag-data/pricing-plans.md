## FAQ

**Q: Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan at any time?**
A: Yes, plan changes take effect immediately. Upgrades are pro-rated, and downgrades apply at the next billing cycle.

**Q: What happens if I exceed my bandwidth limit?**
A: You'll be billed for overage at your plan's overage rate. We'll notify you at 80% and 100% usage.

**Q: Do you offer refunds for unused bandwidth?**
A: Bandwidth packages are non-refundable but roll over for up to 3 months.

**Q: Can I have multiple users on one account?**
A: Professional plans include 3 team members. Enterprise plans have unlimited team members with role-based access.

**Q: Is there a setup fee?**
A: No setup fees for Starter or Professional plans. Enterprise custom deployments may include professional services fees.
